The sun rises.
You have rested well, despite the recent raid.

You do your improvised morning routines.
Wash yourself. Eat whatever you have and see. Check the sword ol' gramps gave to you.
Take a look around, stretch. Think about which way to go.

As you get ready... you hear the walking noises. You get startled, take sword and investigate.

But it's not what it seems. You encounter a friendly, wandering halfling trader.

"Hi! I see you're in a heightened state of sense... would you want to get this cheer-up potion for 5 pogs?"
You think about it, and check your pockets. Indeed, you have exactly 5 pogs to trade for the cheer-up potion.

So begins the trade phase...
